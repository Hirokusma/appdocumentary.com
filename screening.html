WWDC Screening

We are holding and early screening of App at WWDC and we want you to be able to see it—even if you can't be in San Jose.

The idea is to set a threshold and, if there is enough interest, hire some pros to produce a live online stream of the event.

If you're interested, you can pre-order your streaming ticket for $15. For your support, we will include a copy of the finished film when it comes out. If we can hit 600 pre-orders, we will then (and only then) bill everyone and move forward with the live stream.

If that sounds like something you're interested in, add your email address and we will notify you in a week or so when we have the pre-order system built.
